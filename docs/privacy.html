OTPilot – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2025

No data collection

OTPilot does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (account names, TOTP secrets, and target URLs) is stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's local storage API and never leaves your browser.

No external servers

OTPilot does not communicate with any external server. There are no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party services integrated into the extension.

Permissions used

  • storage — saves your account configuration locally on your device.
  • tabs — opens the Ko-fi support page when you click "Buy me a coffee."
  • host permissions (*://*/*) — allows the extension to detect and fill OTP fields on any site you configure as a target.
